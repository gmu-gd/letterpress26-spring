Exercise 1

Metal Type

Description

Together with your group, come up with a group name and set each of your names in metal type (reference video). The typeface is up to you but should be something larger than 24pt. The names do not have to all be the same typeface or size.

Requirements

  • Method - Metal Type
  • Inking - Hand
  • Paper - Letter (Provided)
  • Print Area - Considered
  • Color - 1
  • Passes - 1
  • Amount - 20 (per group)

Things to Keep in Mind

  • Wash your hands after handling metal type
  • The relationship of each name to the other names and where they exist on the page
  • Think beyond your first idea
  • Setting up takes more time than the actual printing
  • Be clean and precise

Objectives

  • Learning type and printing terminology
  • Building set up and clean up skills
  • Working cooperatively
  • Building printing skills

Final Submission

  • Turned in on time (2/16/26)
  • Twenty physical prints
  • Photographs of five best submitted on Canvas
  • All other requirements met
