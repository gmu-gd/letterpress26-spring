Project 2

Broadside

Description

This project will combine laser cutting, letterpress printing, and risograph printing to design and print a gig poster for an event of your choosing.

The poster needs to include at least the name of the artist, date of the event, and location of the event (both venue and city/state) but can include more. The design of the poster is up to you but it needs to be in at least two colors. The first color will be printed on the Riso and the second color will be printed on the press. More colors are possible but add time and bring in more complexity with registration.

Getting your laser cut to be type high will be done in class and there will be a small material cost for this project.

Requirements

  • Method - Riso + Laser Cut Wood Plate
  • Inking - Hand
  • Paper - 12"x18" (Provided)
  • Print Area - 10"x16"
  • Color - 2+ (at least 1 on Riso, 1 on Press)
  • Passes - 2+ (at least 1 on Riso, 1 on Press)
  • Amount - 20

Things to Keep in Mind

  • Think about the strengths of each process.
  • Keep fine detail and type to a minimum for the laser cut
  • Laser cutting will take time.
  • Look at the colors available on the Riso
  • This is a communal space so treat it as such.
  • Prints should be identical.
  • Work intuitively, be curious, and create intentionally.

Objectives

  • Learn a new process with the Riso
  • Building technical skill while printing
  • Improving skill on press
  • Improving craft skills
  • Consider the edition
  • Working on registration
