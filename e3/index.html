Exercise 3

Hand Cut Chipboard

Description

Create a print using cut chipboard. The content of the print is up to you. You can layer other student's cut chipboard on your print but keep at least 10 as your own.

Requirements

  • Method - Chipboard
  • Inking - Hand
  • Paper - Letter (Provided)
  • Print Area - at least 1 inch margins
  • Color - 1+
  • Passes - 1
  • Amount - 20

Things to Keep in Mind

  • Chipboard is a single use printing material
  • Think beyond your first idea
  • Setting up takes more time than the actual printing
  • Be clean and precise

Objectives

  • Creating printable things quickly
  • Building set up and clean up skills
  • Working cooperatively
  • Building printing skills

Final Submission

  • Turned in on time (3/2/26)
  • 20 prints
  • Photograph of one print submitted on Canvas
  • All other requirements met
